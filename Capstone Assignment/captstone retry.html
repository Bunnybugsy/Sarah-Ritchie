The Path To SEL


This website is tailored to assist teachers in facilitating classroom activities, lessons, and learning experiences to enhance children's understanding of healthy social and emotional processes, coping mechanisms, and strategies. The goal is to contribute to improved outcomes in students' social, emotional, academic, and behavioral development.

As the designer of this website, my aspiration is that this platform will promote a quick, seamless, and effective integration of Social Emotional Learning (SEL) in a junior classroom. However, it's important to note that all activities provided are adaptable and suitable for students ranging from primary to senior grades.

I aim to challenge teachers, caregivers, staff, candidates, and parents to recognize SEL as a valuable tool for children to embrace as early as possible. This curriculum should be viewed as a gateway for teachers to explore and introduce SEL processes that they may not have considered before.

Lets ROLL Right Into The Curriculum Guide

According to numerous researchers and SEL experts, there are five established core pillars of SEL. Lesson plans, activities, and resources provided on the website will follow the guidance of these core competencies to produce the most optimal classroom outcomes. The goal of SEL and this program's design is to improve individuals' ability to access and use skills honed by these five essential pillars, translating them throughout their development to acquire healthy coping, processing, and behavioral, social, and emotional skills. It is within my own beliefs that SEL is best facilitated in a classroom environment, as a teacher can provide consistency and organize skill-based training that builds off one another to gauge deep understanding and skill in the SEL atmosphere.
